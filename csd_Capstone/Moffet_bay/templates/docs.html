Moffat Bay Resort Documentation

Introduction

Welcome to the Moffat Bay Resort documentation. This guide provides all the information you need to navigate and utilize our website effectively.

Getting Started

To begin, explore our resort's offerings on the home page. For more details about our services, visit the about page.

Registration

To access exclusive deals and make reservations, please register an account:

  1. Navigate to the Sign Up page.
  2. Fill in the required personal and contact information.
  3. Submit the form to create your account.

Login

If you already have an account, log in to access your profile and manage reservations:

  1. Go to the Sign In page.
  2. Enter your credentials.
  3. Click the login button to access your account.

Making a Reservation

Follow these steps to reserve a room:

  1. Log in to your account.
  2. Visit the Reserve a Room page.
  3. Select your desired room type and dates.
  4. Confirm your reservation details and submit.

After logging in, the user can check for:

  1. Search for reservations by Reservation Number, Last Name, or Email.
  2. Visit the Reserve a Room page.

Contact Information

If you have any questions or need assistance, please reach out through our contact page.
